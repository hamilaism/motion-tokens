Motion tokens — Storybook prototypes

Two complementary prototypes built during research for Chapter 25 of The Design Tokens Book. Both demonstrate a motion token system organized around a two-axis model (abstraction level × composition depth).

Prototype 1

System by system

Token demos organized by category, with reproductions of nine benchmarked design systems and cross-system comparison stories.
Prototype 2

Concept by concept

The same systems appear as evidence for architectural decisions — composition depth, intent segmentation, naming architecture, spring approach, scale design.
